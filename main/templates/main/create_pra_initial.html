Create new Personal Risk Assessment form

This form should be completed by the line manager after the risk assessment discussion has taken place with the member of staff, or contractor.

Once the form has been submitted, the member of staff will be sent a copy and invited to confirm its accuracy, and to give consent for the information processed in line with the privacy notice. It will then go to the nominated SCS to approve.
