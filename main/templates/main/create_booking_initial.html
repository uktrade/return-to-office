Lateral flow test

DIT encourages anyone who must go into an office and who does not have symptoms of COVID-19 to volunteer for a lateral flow test at a testing site.

If you work at a DIT London office, then you can book an appointment at the civil service workplace asymptomatic testing site at the QEII Conference Centre just off Parliament Square.

If you regularly need to attend the office, from 2 to 5 days a week, we recommend testing twice a week with at least 48 hours between tests.

If you need to attend for an occasional visit, we recommend you get a test on the day of attendance, before attending the office if possible.

You can read our guidance on booking a lateral flow test.
